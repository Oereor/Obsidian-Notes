1. $\displaystyle \int \frac{dx}{x^2-a^2}=\frac{1}{2a}\ln\left|\frac{x-a}{x+a}\right|+C$ $(a\neq 0)$ 

2. $\displaystyle \int\frac{dx}{\sqrt{x^2\pm a^2}}=\ln|x^2+\sqrt{x^2\pm a^2}|+C$

3. $\displaystyle \int \tan x\,dx = -\ln|\cos x| + C = \ln|\sec x| + C$

4. $\displaystyle \int\sec x\,dx=\ln|\sec x+\tan x|+C$

5. $\displaystyle \int \csc x\,dx=-\ln|\csc x+\cot x|+C$

6. $\displaystyle \int\sqrt{a^2-x^2}\,dx=\frac{a^2}{2}\arcsin\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)+\frac{1}{2}x\sqrt{a^2-x^2}+C$

7. $\displaystyle \int e^{ax}\cos(bx)\,dx=\frac{e^{ax}}{a^2+b^2}(a\cos(bx)+b\sin(bx))+C$

8. $\displaystyle \int\sqrt{x^2+a^2}\,dx=\frac{x\sqrt{x^2+a^2}}{2}+\frac{a^2}{2}\ln\left|x+\sqrt{x^2+a^2}\right|+C$

9. $\displaystyle \int\sqrt{x^2-a^2}\,dx=\frac{x\sqrt{x^2-a^2}}{2}-\frac{a^2}{2}\ln\left|x+\sqrt{x^2-a^2}\right|+C$

### 常用的三角换元方法

1. $\sqrt{x^2+a^2}$, 则令 $x=a\tan t$. 
2. $\sqrt{x^2-a^2}$, 则令 $x=a\sec t$.
3. $\sqrt{a^2 - x^2}$, 则令 $x=a\sin t$.

### 某个奇怪的例题

求解不定积分: $\displaystyle \int\frac{1-\ln x}{(x-\ln x)^2}\,dx$

**解.** $$\begin{align*}
    \int\frac{1-\ln x}{(x-\ln x)^2}\,dx&=\int\frac{x(\frac{1}{x}-1)}{(x-\ln x)^2}\,dx+\int\frac{1}{x-\ln x}\,dx\\
    &=-\int\frac{x}{(x-\ln x)^2}\,d(x-\ln x)+\int\frac{1}{x-\ln x}\,dx\\
    &=\int x\,d\left( \frac{1}{x-\ln x} \right)+\int\frac{1}{x-\ln x}\,dx\\
    &=\frac{x}{x-\ln x} - \int \frac{1}{x-\ln x} \, dx + \int \frac{1}{x-\ln x} \, dx \\
    &=\frac{x}{x-\ln x} + C
\end{align*}$$
